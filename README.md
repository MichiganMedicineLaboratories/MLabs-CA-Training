Welcome to the Michigan Medicine Laboratories Slide and Block Kit Training repository!

This resource provides comprehensive training materials designed to ensure a smooth and effective consultation process for specimen submissions. Here, you’ll find detailed information about our kits, their contents, and an organized checklist of required and optional materials and documentation.

Our training empowers clients—and MLabs staff alike—to deliver exceptional patient care by streamlining the submission process. By participating in this course, clients will gain the knowledge needed to include all necessary materials and documents when submitting specimens for consultation with Michigan Medicine Laboratories.

Let's work together to make every consultation seamless and impactful!
